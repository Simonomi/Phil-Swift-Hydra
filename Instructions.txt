Put the phils folder in the startup folder (folder hidden, not contents)
Put hydra.vbs on the desktop
Make shortcut to hydra.vbs in startup folder then hide hydra.vbs (not shortcut)
Restart computer and enjoy!

Note:
To get to startup folder, press windows + r, then type in "shell:startup" and press enter
To hid files, right click > properties > check "hidden"
To make a shortcut, right click > send to > desktop (create shortcut)
